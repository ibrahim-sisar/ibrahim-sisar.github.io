About

me

Ibrahim Said Abu Al Roos is a Palestinian programmer and innovator from Gaza. My journey in programming and robotics began at the age of 12, driven by a passion for technology and a desire to create smart solutions that improve people's lives.

I have mastered several programming languages, including Python, C, JavaScript, and SQL, and have developed projects in web applications, artificial intelligence, and robotics. I have received local and international awards for my projects, including the WRO Robotics Olympiad and the Scientific and Technological Exhibition, proudly representing Palestine in global competitions.

I was also a TEDx speaker, where I shared my journey and experiences in programming and innovation to inspire young minds and encourage creativity and development.

My goal is to continue learning and innovating, contribute to developing smart technological solutions that serve communities, and leave my mark in the world of technology.
